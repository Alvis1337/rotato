Rotato

Automatic wallpaper rotation for Android, powered by your favourite image sources.

Download APK

Features

Auto Rotation

Set it and forget it — wallpapers change on your schedule, from 15 minutes to once a day.

Plugin Store

Install source plugins from the official store or add your own community index URL.

Collections

Save wallpapers into locked or unlocked collections. Link a collection to the rotation pool.

Schedule

Switch collections automatically on specific days and times.

MAL Integration

Filter wallpapers by your MyAnimeList anime — show only art from shows you love.

Privacy First

No accounts, no analytics, no ads. Everything stays on your device.
