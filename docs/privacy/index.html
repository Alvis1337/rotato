Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2025

Rotato is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or sell data.

Information We Do Not Collect

Rotato does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. There are no analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, or advertising networks in the app.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data generated by Rotato stays on your device:

  • Wallpapers and rotation pool: Images downloaded from third-party sources or imported from your gallery are saved in the app's private storage.
  • Preferences and settings: Your rotation interval, wallpaper target, filter settings, and collection metadata are stored using Android DataStore in app-private storage.
  • Wallpaper history: The last 50 wallpapers set are stored locally for the History screen.
  • MyAnimeList credentials: If you connect a MyAnimeList account, your OAuth access token is stored locally using Android DataStore. It is never sent to our servers.

Network Access

Rotato uses the internet only to fetch wallpaper content from third-party sources you configure. These sources include:

  • Danbooru, Gelbooru, Safebooru (image boards)
  • Wallhaven (wallpaper aggregator)
  • Konachan, Yande.re (anime wallpaper boards)

When fetching wallpapers, your device connects directly to these services. Rotato does not proxy or log these requests. Each service has its own terms of use and privacy policy.

MyAnimeList authentication uses OAuth 2.0 via the official MyAnimeList API. We do not store or log your MAL credentials.

Biometric Authentication

Rotato uses Android's biometric APIs to lock individual wallpaper collections. Biometric data is handled entirely by the Android OS — Rotato never accesses or stores your fingerprint, face data, or any other biometric information.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions and uses them only as described:

  • SET_WALLPAPER — to apply wallpapers to your home and lock screens.
  • INTERNET — to download wallpapers from configured sources.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to show rotation status notifications with quick actions (Skip / Keep).
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — to trigger collection switches at the exact scheduled time.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to restore rotation scheduling after a device reboot.
  • USE_BIOMETRIC — to lock and unlock wallpaper collections with biometric auth.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — for background rotation processing.

Children's Privacy

Rotato is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
