# Privacy Policy for Rotato

**Last updated: April 11, 2026**

Rotato is a wallpaper rotation app for Android. This policy describes what data the app does and does not collect.

## Data Collection

Rotato does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

- **No account required** — the app has no login or registration.
- **No network access** — the app makes no internet connections. Your photos never leave your device.
- **No analytics or crash reporting** — no third-party SDKs are used for tracking or telemetry.
- **No advertising** — the app contains no ads.

## Photos

When you add photos to Rotato, copies are saved to the app's private storage on your device (`/data/data/com.chrisalvis.rotato/`). These copies are only accessible to the app and are deleted when you remove photos from the rotation pool or uninstall the app.

## Permissions

Rotato requests one permission:

- **Set Wallpaper** (`android.permission.SET_WALLPAPER`) — required to change your device wallpaper. This permission is automatically granted on install and requires no user prompt.

## Open Source

Rotato is open source. The full source code is available at [https://github.com/chrisalvis/rotato](https://github.com/chrisalvis/rotato).
